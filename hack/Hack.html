ABOUT ME

I love Designing & Fashion

As a Graphic designer, I strive to create designs that are not only visually appealing but also communicate a message effectively. With 3 years of experience in the industry, I have worked with clients from various sectors, such as fashion, food.

My areas of expertise include logo design, print design and Photography. I take pride in my ability to deliver projects on time and on budget, while maintaining the highest standards of quality.

The fashion industry is incredibly harmful to the planet. Second only to oil, the industry is one of the world’s largest polluters, responsible for 20% of global industrial water pollution. It comes as a surprise to many that most clothes are actually made out of plastic, creating a microplastic disaster in the making.

Add to that the thousands of harmful chemicals used in the textile mills around the world, which are dangerous to both the environment and the people working with them, and the time has come for a global change in the industry. That change must come in the form of sustainable fashion.

Let me set the scene!
