Monetisation

Customise your product

Assign monetisation incentives to your product's digital ID. Customise your own labels or use the pre-built labels inside to tag products with unique offerings, such as discount offers, free services, or special promotions.

Customers can access each product's utility by either scanning the digital ID on their physical product or next to the item in their shopping cart.

Customise Your Utility Label

How It Works

Once you have customized or selected a label, you can assign it to the product's digital ID. Customers will then be able to unlock these utilities by scanning the digital ID on the product or in their shopping cart.

Make sure to test the digital ID on sample products before rolling out to your entire inventory to ensure that the utilities are functioning as expected.
